My Life

2025-Present: Product @ UE Labs, UserEvidence

2024-2025: CEO & Co-Founder, Zealot (Acquired by UserEvidence)

Founded Zealot at 23. Zealot is the Customer Proof Platform that helps B2B companies like Cisco, Airtable, ClickUp, and Carta eliminate buyer skepticism at every stage of the funnel.

Started with a $45 investment, initially building a DTC Shopify plugin that served ecom brands with $1-3K yearly ACV. I built and sold the initial product in Figma during Econ class for $499/mo. In 2024, I pivoted the company to serve underserved B2B companies, raised venture capital, and scaled average ACV from $3K/yr to $30K/yr, eventually signing $1M+ deals with enterprise customers like Cisco, Airtable, ClickUp, Carta, and Yubico.

Then led the M&A effort receiving inbound interest from multiple players, creating a competitive bidding process, flying out to meet with acquirers, leading the company through diligence/legal, etc. Eventually scaled the company to an upper 7-figure acquisition by UserEvidence in 18 months.

As CEO & Co-Founder, I wore every hat - product manager, sales leader, GTM strategist, customer success, product marketing, late-night coding sessions, pitch deck iterations, and everything in between.
